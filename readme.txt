LinkFive 

PRESENTATION

LinkFive is a plugin that collect the last 5 posts from your LinkedIn to publish it on your website. 

NOTICE

=> First step

	_ Click on LinkFive in worpress menu and click on " Login ".
	_ it's here you will connect to your LinkedIn account.

=> Second step

	_ Once your LinkedIn is registered, you can observe your account on LinkFive's page. 
	_ Copy the shortcode to paste it on your Wordpress page.
Enjoy!
